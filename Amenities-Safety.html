Safety Features

Fire Prevention
To prevent house fires we have...
An alarm to notify the homeowner
Sprinklers in the walls and ceiling
Mechanical rats that dispense water
Robots that have a green fire retardent at the ready
Finally, we have 20 hoses that have their own green mixture to extinguish any and all fires that go beyond the strength of the previous measures
Home Security
In the event of any invaders, human or not, we have...
A password locked front door, sound/voice recognition locks
Automatic shudders to scare away animals
finally shades on all possible entrances to hide and protect the homeowner
