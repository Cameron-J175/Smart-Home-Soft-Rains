Safety Features

Fire Prevention
To prevent fire with all of the technology in the house we have and alarm to notify the homeowner, sprinklers in the walls and ceiling, mechanical rats that dispense water robots that have a green fire retardent at the ready, and finally 20 hoses that have their own green froth mixture to extinguish any and all fires
Home Security
In the event of any invaders, human or not, we have a password locked front door, sound recognition, automatic shudders to scare away animals, and finally shades on all possible entrances to hide and protect the homeowner
